Welcome to the Scene Sketch Dataset Collection Application


Please carefully read each set of instructions. You'll be given a series of questions that you must answer correctly to continue with the next page. Failure to answer all questions correctly will result in redirection to the information page.

Instructions:

  • On the upcoming pages, you will receive a scene description and a drawing canvas.

  • You are asked to draw a scene by referring to the description.

  • To switch from drawing mode to erasing mode, simply click the button located on the top-left corner.

  • You should describe your scene verbally in English while drawing.

  • We will record your drawing canvas and verbal descriptions via Zoom meeting.

  • You are allowed to add extra objects to your drawing, even if they are not mentioned in the description.

  • You have 3 mins to draw your scene. When the time is over, you will be directed to the next scene automatically.
