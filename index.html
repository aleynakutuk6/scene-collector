Welcome to the Scene Sketch Drawing App

Please thoroughly review each set of instructions. You will encounter a sequence of questions that require correct answers to proceed to the next page. Any incorrect responses will lead to redirection to the information page.

Instructions

  • On the upcoming pages, you will receive a scene description and a drawing canvas.

  • Your task is to create a visual representation of the scene by following the provided description.

  • To switch between drawing and erasing modes, simply click the "ERASER" button.

  • DO NOT draw the next object before finishing the current one.

  • While drawing, verbally describe your scene in English.

  • We will capture your drawing canvas and your verbal descriptions through Zoom recording.

  • Feel free to include additional objects in your drawing, even if they are not mentioned in the scene description.

  • You will have one and a half minute to complete your scene. Once the time is up, you will be automatically redirected to the next scene.
